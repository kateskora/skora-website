The Skora brand

Skora takes its cue from the Vikings, a core part of founder Kate's family history. The Vikings knew who they were and influenced the culture of every place they went, utilising the best of both worlds to create future value. We bring that same spirit to our clients' projects: challenging the status quo, questioning assumptions, and pairing deep technical experience with the courage to do things differently. The word 'skora' means to challenge in Old Norse and we feel it fits who we are perfectly!

Who we are

Seasoned practitioners who bring a valuable and proven blend of expertise.

Kate Gelsthorpe (was Hobbs) Founder and Principal LinkedIn

Kate is a well-respected leader who unites cross-functional teams and levels to achieve significant organisational change. She partners with executive leadership to navigate complex transitions and establish organisations that are both resilient and responsive.

Bringing more than twenty years' experience from operational, project and consulting positions within the resources industry and other sectors, she is valued for her curiosity and realistic perspective, dedication to solutions that address underlying organisational needs, and talent for encouraging creativity and professional growth in demanding circumstances.

Kate's qualifications include degrees in Marketing and International Studies, a Master's in Mining Engineering, Lean Six Sigma Black Belt certification, AICD membership, and Fellowship of the SA Leadership Institute's Governor's Leadership Foundation. She is a Fellow of the Australasian Institute of Mining and Metallurgy (AusIMM).

Beyond restructuring governance and operational systems across numerous organisations, Kate has overseen the strategic reassessment of significant mining ventures including major expansion and feasibility studies that unlocked long-term investment decisions and mine-life extensions.

Named one of the 100 Global Inspirational Women in Mining and a recipient of the AusIMM Minerals Sector Service Award, Kate is committed to amplifying underrepresented voices and advancing diversity and inclusion initiatives throughout the sector.

Process

Our approach in action

We start by listening, then we build momentum together.

01
Diagnosis

Understanding your reality

We spend time exploring critical artefacts, how you work, and talking to key stakeholders to build a clear picture of your current reality. This provides a fresh-eyes look at existing dynamics and highlights opportunities that may not have been visible before.

02
Design

Shaping the path forward

Together we map options, test assumptions, and commit to a process of delivery. We focus on scoping both the problem and the process, as assuming the solution is clear before getting into the work can sometimes have you solving the wrong thing.

03
Delivery

Making change stick in your operation

We work with you in a way that suits your context and desired level of engagement, adjusting as conditions shift and results emerge. This gives you the expert support where you need it and helps build internal capability along the way, so any change holds long after we step back.
